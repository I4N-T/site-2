Software Development

I'm not going to bother going into much detail on March, April, and May. The stats speak for theirselves. I'll just say that I am overall dissappointed in how unproductive I was in the first half of the year. I have only to look forward to what the future holds in store, and I am optimistic.

Now let's talk about June. Although the hours logged looks just as bad as the previous three months, there is a silver lining. For the last half of the month, especially in the last week, I have been on a hot streak. 57.00 out of the total 69.00 logged software dev hours were logged in the last half of the month. Even that is short of my aspirations, but I'd say it's not too bad.

I am currently enrolled in Intro to Information Security for the summer semester. So far, I am mostly pleased with the class material. I have especially enjoyed learning about and getting some good hands-on experience with cryptography.

Aside from that, I have recently gotten back to sharpening my Android development skills. Currently, I am working through this fun tutorial on how to build a social networking app using Firebase as the backend. Hopefully, I will be able to use what I'm learning here to build my own social networking app as a personal project.

Fitness

In June 2020, my overall fitness hours totalled to 15.00 hours. I spent 6.00 hours lifting weights, and I spent 9.00 hours on cardio. My average lifting hours per week was 1.40 hours, and my average cardio hours per week was 2.10 hours.

Note that "lifting weights" here really just means any anaerobic exercise. Due to the pandemic, the local gym has been closed for a while. It has recently re-opened, but I do not personally feel that it is safe to return just yet. In lieu of weight lifting, I have been doing mostly bodyweight anaerobic exercises such as (multiple variations of) push-ups and pull-ups. I do have some dumbells at home, so I have been doing some minor weight lifting, namely shoulder presses, shrugs, and goblet squats. A local park, Oriole Park to be exact, has some outdoor exercise equipment, so I have been going there for my workouts occasionally.

Also, I know that 15.00 total fitness hours for the month does not look good. However, 13.00 out of those 15.00 hours are from just the last half of the month. In fact, I have taken zero days off in over two weeks. On June 30, I weighed in at 191.8 lbs (a healthy weight for me), so at least I can't say that my laziness over the past several months has had all that much impact on my health.
